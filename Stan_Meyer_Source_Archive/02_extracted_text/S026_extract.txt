In conventional step-up transformers, the voltage developed across the secondary winding depends on the ratio of the number of turns between the primary andsecondary windings. Other factors are the size of the windings and whether the core is made of air or magnetic material.
Now, the basic principle of the present invention is to use a particle accelerator, and the basic principle is to induce a voltage in the secondary winding by passingmagnetic particles through the secondary winding. be.
The device of the present invention includes a primary groove winding having a magnetically formed core and a low voltage input. The secondary winding has a largernumber of turns than the primary winding, and has an output for utilizing the voltage induced in the secondary winding.
Thus, the primary visiting winding and its core are disposed at one location on the non-magnetic motherboard which is endless (i.e. forming a closed loop), while thesecondary winding is It is arranged around the endless mother eve at a position opposite to the primary winding.
The pipe is filled with magnetic elements having a magnetically distributed charge. This magnetic element is a particle,Preferably, the gas is used.
The magnetic particles due to polarization move, and the particles move through the accelerator (the firstWhen approaching the next winding), the magnetic field of the device attracts the particles,The particles are accelerated and introduced into the space inside the coil. As the magnetic elements (particles) advance, the repulsive end of the accelerator exerts afurther magnetic force on the particles. The magnetic attraction and repulsion significantly strengthens the motion of each particle in the pipe. . L, the particle leaves theregion of the accelerator with increased velocity.
Due to the action of the accelerator, the magnetic element moves in a closed loop at a speed that is significantly greater than its normal motion. When the magneticelement passes through the core of the secondary winding, theA voltage is induced in the next winding.
In this way, a fairly large voltage is induced in the secondary winding of the transformer.
The main object of the invention is to provide a power generation device capable of generating voltage/current values significantly greater than those that were otherwisepossible.
Another object of the invention is to provide such a power generation device and a magnetic accelerator device using magnetized elements.
Another object of the present invention is to provide a power generation device that can control the amplitude of the output.
Another object of the present invention is to provide a power generation device that can be used with direct current, alternating current, pulsed or other waveforms.
Another object of the present invention is to provide a power generation device that can be used in single-phase or three-phase electrical systems.
Still another object of the present invention is to provide a generator for generating magnetized particles used in an electric particle accelerator.
Still another object of the present invention is to provide a power generation device that can be easily implemented using easily available components.
